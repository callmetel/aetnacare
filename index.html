In the care system most of us have come to know, we can all have trouble understanding how to manage our conditions.

AetnaCare completely reimagines the approach to care. It’s a personal, coordinated program that accounts for everything that impacts our health:

  • Advanced analytics that identify when someone’s at-risk

  • Local nurses who provide one-on-one support

  • Evidence-based guidelines to best manage conditions

  • Innovative, community-based resources to support good health

With AetnaCare, we can gain the knowledge, skills and confidence we need to take an active role in our health journeys.

How do we identify the members who need us most?

With real-time and predictive analytics.

How do we identify the members
who need us most?

With real-time and predictive analytics.

When we need support, we might ask for help. But more often than not, we need someone to pinpoint those of us who most need guidance.

AetnaCare leverages advanced algorithms and comprehensive patient data to identify those who are disengaged. Our system anticipates when our conditions could progress. And it proactively alerts our nurses if we’re at risk to become hospitalized in the near future.

With these insights, AetnaCare nurses are able to work with our doctors. And together, they can give us the support we need.

How do we connect with our members?

With care nurses who live in their community.

How do we connect with our members?

With care nurses who live in their community.

If we’re struggling to manage our health, we need someone who can put us on a better path. Local AetnaCare nurses are our guides and advocates. They work with our doctors, families and the many collaborators in the health ecosystem to ensure the coordination of our care.

From visiting us at home to accompanying us to our doctor’s appointments, AetnaCare nurses:

  • Assess our needs and opportunities for improvement

  • Educate us to better manage our conditions

  • Motivate us to take a more active role in our health

With this collaborative approach, AetnaCare ensures that our care is simple, clear and organized. And it helps us become more active in our health journeys.

How do we advise our members?

With guidelines specific to each member and their condition.

How do we advise our members?

With guidelines specific to each member and their condition.

No two patients are the same, yet care is often one-size-fits-all. Not with AetnaCare.

We follow structured, evidence-based guidelines that are both condition-specific and personalized to our individual needs. Our AetnaCare nurses use them to guide us throughout our journeys. And they can set tailored health goals to help us manage our conditions on our own.

Structure and guidance give us the confidence we need to make healthy choices, whether taking our medications consistently or taking advantage of social services in our communities.
